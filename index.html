Cinnamon Rolls

a recipe without the need of mixer.

Ingredients

for about 4 pieces

  • bread flour 150g
  • milk 102g
  • unsalted butter 26g
  • granulated sugar 20g
  • instant dry yeast 1/2 tsp
  • salt 2g
  • black sugar 30g
  • soften unsalted butter 40g
  • cinnamon power 2.5g

Steps.

Dough

1. Add granulated sugar(20g), unsalted butter(26g) and whole milk(102g) to a pot.

2. Gently stir over low heat until melting. Then cool down to 37-40 Celsius.

3. Add instant dry yeast(1/2 tsp) into the pot. Wait for about 10 minutes.

4. Add bread flour(150g) and salt(2g) to the pot and mix well.

5. Cover with olastic wrap, let it rise for about 1 hour.(We need to dough become twice as big as the original dough.)

Filling

1. Combine the suger and cinnamon.

2. Add soften unsalted butter to the combination and mix until well combined.

3. After rising, knead the dough for 10 mins until smooth and elastic.

4. Roll the dough into a thin rectangle

5. Spread the filling on to the dough evenly and roll the dough.

6. Cut the roll into 3.5cm thick.

7. Put all 4 cinnamon rolls into a pan and let them rise for another 30 minutes.

8. Preheat the oven to 180 Celsius, back for 30minutes until golden brown.

9. Take out from the oven and let cool down for 10 minutes.
